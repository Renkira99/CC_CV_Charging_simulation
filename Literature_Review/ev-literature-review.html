🎯 Step 3

How to Find Recent + Highly Cited

1

Paste query into Google Scholar

Use any of the search queries above — just hit the copy button!

2

Click "Since 2022" or "Since 2023"

Left sidebar → Custom range. This filters out old papers instantly.

3

Check "Cited by X" under each result

50+ citations for 2023-2024 = great. 200+ for older = foundational.

4

Click "Cited by" to find newer related papers

This is a goldmine — papers that cite a good paper are usually also good!

5

Get full PDF via IEEE/ScienceDirect or ResearchGate

Use uni login for IEEE/Elsevier. Check ResearchGate for free author uploads.

🧠 Reference

Key Concepts Cheat Sheet

Charging

CC-CV Charging

Constant Current → Constant Voltage. Current stays flat, then voltage caps and current tapers off.

Charging

Charging Levels

L1: 1.4kW (slow) · L2: 7-19kW (moderate) · L3/DC Fast: 50-350kW (fast)

Charging

Converter Topologies

Vienna Rectifier · LLC Resonant · Full-Bridge · Interleaved Buck/Boost

Charging

V2G / V2V

Vehicle-to-Grid & Vehicle-to-Vehicle. Bidirectional power flow using the EV battery.

Harmonics

THD

Total Harmonic Distortion — measures waveform distortion as % of fundamental frequency.

Harmonics

IEEE 519

The standard for harmonic limits at the Point of Common Coupling (PCC). Must comply.

Harmonics

Active Power Filter

Real-time harmonic compensation. Injects anti-harmonics to cancel out distortion.

Harmonics

PFC (Power Factor Correction)

Reduces harmonic content and improves power factor. Common in modern chargers.
